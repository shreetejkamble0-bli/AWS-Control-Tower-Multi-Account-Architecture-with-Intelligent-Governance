Management Account

The Management Account is the central administration account in AWS Control Tower. It is responsible for governance, billing, account management, security policies, and overall control of the AWS organization.

Organization Management

Manages AWS Organizations, creates accounts, and controls organizational units (OUs).

Billing & Cost Control

Centralized billing, cost monitoring, budgeting, and financial governance.

Policy Management

Applies Service Control Policies (SCPs) and governance rules across all accounts.

Account Provisioning

Creates and manages new AWS accounts using AWS Control Tower Account Factory.

Key Responsibilities

  • Manage AWS Organization structure.
  • Configure security and compliance guardrails.
  • Monitor organization-wide costs and usage.
  • Control account creation and lifecycle management.
  • Maintain centralized governance across AWS environments.
  • Provide visibility into resources and compliance status.
