Production Accounts

Production Accounts host live applications, customer-facing services, databases, and business-critical workloads. These accounts are designed for high availability, security, scalability, and operational excellence.

Live Applications

Run customer-facing web applications, APIs, and enterprise systems.

High Availability

Ensure continuous service availability through redundancy and failover.

Scalability

Automatically scale infrastructure to handle varying workloads.

Security Controls

Protect sensitive data using monitoring, encryption, and access controls.

Disaster Recovery

Maintain backup and recovery strategies for business continuity.

24/7 Monitoring

Continuously monitor application performance and operational health.

Key Benefits of Production Accounts

  • Supports business-critical workloads.
  • Provides secure and compliant environments.
  • Ensures high availability and reliability.
  • Offers automated monitoring and alerting.
  • Enables disaster recovery and backup strategies.
  • Delivers scalable infrastructure for growth.
